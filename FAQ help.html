How do I create an account on FastTravel?

▼
Creating an account is simple! Click the "Get Started" button on our homepage, then select "Register." Fill in your details, verify your email, and you're ready to go.

What documents do I need to rent or buy a car?

▼
For renting: Valid driver's license, proof of insurance, and a credit card.
For buying: Valid ID, proof of income, insurance information, and necessary payment documents.
